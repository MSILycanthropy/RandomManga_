About RNGmanga.com

Hello! I'm Ethan, otherwise and more preferably known as my online moniker, Lycanthropy, I'm the creator of this site!

I noticed there was a huge lack in terms of manga/manhwa/manhua recommendation, and I figured I'd fill that gap with an ad-free site. So that people can get access to the large array of comics that exist!

This site takes heavy inspiriation from RandomAnime created by Kyle Hawks. Their site is far better than mine probably ever will be, so please go check it out. It's honestly incredible, and it is also ad free!

Over the past few months I've shifted almost exclusively to reading manga, but couldn't find a place for good recommendations, so I figured I'd make it. And I am constantly working it as well as other projects. So please keep an eye out for any updates and make sure to send me feedback.

Any feedback you send using the form below or at the feedback form goes directly to my email and I make sure to look over every one of them! Similarly if you notice any errors or discrepancies in the data, or wish to suggest more comics for the site, please go over to the reports section.

Places to read!

If possible always read from a source that supports the creators. I understand that this is not always possible due to translations, or other issues. But, still use less legal sites at your own risk.

Just a note, I don't particularly read a lot of Manhua specifically, so I'm not aware of the legit sites for them. So, if you are someone who reads lots of Manhua, please let me know if there are any sites you would like added!

Second note, I only speak English, so please let me know of non-English sites, or anything I can do to support non-English speakers, please let me know as well.
