How does it work?

Just fill out the form below and click generate to get a randomly generated list that tries to match the genres you listed as best as possible. If no genres are listed, then they are just listed at random.
Here's a quick explanation of what each form field does, and how to use them

  • Type: The type of comic by country of origin. Either manga (JP), manhwa (KR) or manhua (CN/TW).
  • Minimum Score: The minimum average score of the manga.
  • Amount to List: Self-explanatory. The amount to show in the list!
  • Completed: Whether or not only completed manga should show up.
  • Include Unscored: Whether or not to include manga without any score data.
  • Genres: Click to open and close, pick the genres you wish to include/exclude in the search.
